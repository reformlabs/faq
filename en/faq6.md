### How do I report a security vulnerability?

If you discover a potential security vulnerability in one of our projects, we strongly encourage you to report it as soon as possible so it can be investigated and addressed. Security issues should be reported through the **GitHub repository related to the affected project**. You can do this by opening a **GitHub Issue** describing the vulnerability.

When reporting a security vulnerability, please try to include as much relevant information as possible, such as:

- The affected project or repository
- The version or commit where the issue occurs
- Steps required to reproduce the vulnerability
- The potential impact or risk of the issue
- Screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept examples if available
- Possible mitigation or fixes (optional)

Providing clear and detailed information helps the Reform Labs team understand the issue more quickly and determine the appropriate fix.

Please avoid submitting vague or incomplete reports, as they may slow down the investigation process.

Once a report is received, our team will review the issue, assess its severity, and determine the appropriate course of action.
