### Can I work with Reform Labs?
We appreciate the interest in collaborating with **Reform Labs**. Many people ask if they can directly join or work with our team.

Currently, **Reform Labs does not accept direct applications, recruitment forms, or open join requests** for the core team. Our team grows organically. We usually invite collaborators based on:

- Prior contributions to our projects
- Demonstrated technical skills and reliability
- Alignment with our project philosophy and development style
- Previous interaction within our ecosystem or community

This approach helps us maintain a **small, focused, and highly collaborative team**.

### How can I get involved?

Even though we don't have a direct application process, you can still engage with our work:

- Contribute to our open-source projects
- Report issues or suggest improvements
- Participate in discussions around our tools and ecosystem

Active contributors and community members are often the people we consider when expanding the team.

### Summary

You cannot currently apply directly to join Reform Labs.  
However, meaningful contributions and community involvement are the best way to become part of our ecosystem.
