### How can I submit a project idea to Reform Labs?

We appreciate people who want to share ideas and help improve the Reform Labs ecosystem. Currently, Reform Labs does not maintain a formal submission process for external project proposals. Our projects are typically initiated and developed internally by the core team. However, if you have an idea that you believe could benefit the ecosystem, you can still share it with us by:

- Participating in community conversations around our projects
- Creating a support request through the **support channel in the Help Center category** of our Discord community server

When sharing an idea, it helps to include:

- A clear description of the problem you want to solve
- The proposed solution or concept
- How it might fit within the Reform Labs ecosystem
- Any early prototypes or examples (if available)

Please note that submitting an idea **does not guarantee that it will be adopted or developed by Reform Labs**. Our team evaluates suggestions based on relevance, feasibility, and alignment with our long-term goals.

Even if a proposal is not adopted, we encourage people to experiment and build independent projects inspired by our work.
